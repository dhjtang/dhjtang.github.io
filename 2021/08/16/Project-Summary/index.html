Project-Summary

2021-08-16 Guestbook

What is my project?

My project is to develop a Matlab-based code to specifically calculate the overall trajectory of the Frisbee. The code calculates all the aerodynamic forces that the Frisbee will be encountering like lift and drag. The code goes deeply into the calculation of every point for each trajectory. After the creation of the code, I was then able to run 3 different experiments on the different aspects of the throw. The different experiments relied on the change of angle of the throw, the change in the diameter, and the change in initial velocity of the Frisbee. Finally, with Matlab’s plotting capabilities, I made three different figures for each experiment on the maximum distance, maximum height, and the trajectory to show how the parameters alter different aspects of the motion.

What are my research target and research method?

My research target is to calculate the trajectory of the Frisbee based on the fundamental parameters such as initial velocity, initial angle, diameter, and so on.
I derived the aerodynamic equations describing the movement of the Frisbee. I used the Euler’s method to solve the flying trajectory of the flying Frisbee.

What was the biggest challenge that I encountered? How did I overcome it?

The biggest challenge that I faced during my project was the Matlab programming. Before the project, I did not have any programming experiences. I overcame the challenge with the learning online.

What was the most interesting part of my project?

The most interesting part of my project is the force analysis of the flying Frisbee.

What are my research result(s) and conclusion(s)?

With the change of angle, the maximum distance will increase and then decrease. We found that the optimal angle of throw to be 10 degrees. With the change of diameter, the distance decreases as the height increases. And with the change of initial velocity, the distance and height will both increase.
